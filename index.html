About me



In 1995, I was born in Eindhoven, a city in the south of The Netherlands. I grew up an only child, introverted but happy (and a bit spoiled). When I was young, it was my dream to move to the UK one day - and I did end up living in Scotland for a year. It was a great experience and I've taken some of my best photos there (what can I say, Holland just doesn't lend itself to the same kind of scenery shots). Now I'm back home, and figuring out where I'll be headed next!



For as long as I can remember, my father has owned a DSLR camera. When I was about 12 years old, having borrowed said camera fairly regularly to take photos of mainly our two cats, my dad bought himself a new Nikon, and I was lucky enough to get the old one. Ever since, I've been running around with a camera whenever I go someplace exciting, new, or meaningful. I have set up this website to maintain a collection of my favourite shots, and to be able to share them with you.



At 17, I moved abroad to Glasgow, Scotland, to go to university. After a year I decided it wasn't for me, and moved back to The Netherlands, where I'm now studying Computer Science with a minor in psychology. I hope to continue my education in the field of human-technology interaction; I am mainly fascinated by the way our lives are changing in the digital age. Apart from IT and photography, I am also passionate about language and design.
